What is Stable-FAST?

Stable-FAST is a novel action tokenizer designed to resolve critical inference instability in autoregressive Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models. Its predecessor, the FAST tokenizer, processes fixed-langth action chunks, which results in token sequences of widely varying lengths. This variability is a primary source of instability, leading to prediction errors, jerky motions, and ultimately, reduced task success rate.
Stable-FAST inverts this method by partitioning action trajectories into variable-length chunks, each normalized to a consistent level of complexity, thereby producing token sequences with significantly reduced length variance.
